# E-Commerce Sales Analytics Dashboard
**Tools:** Power BI · DAX · Data Visualization  
**Domain:** Retail & E-Commerce  

---

## Business Problem
An e-commerce company operating across multiple markets and countries needs a unified view of its sales performance. The business wants to understand which categories, markets, and regions are driving revenue — and which are underperforming — to prioritize marketing spend and inventory decisions.

## Dataset
- Source: Global E-Commerce Sales Dataset
- Key fields: Sales, Category, Market, Country, State, Ship Mode

## Dashboard Overview
Single-page interactive dashboard with 4 KPI cards and 5 visual breakdowns:

| Visual | Insight |
|---|---|
| Column Chart | Sales by Category — identifies top-performing product lines |
| Pie Chart | Sales by Market — regional contribution breakdown |
| Funnel Chart | Sales by Ship Mode — delivery preference analysis |
| Donut Chart | Sales by Country — country-level revenue distribution |
| Treemap | Sales by State — granular geographic performance |

**Slicers:** Dynamic filters for interactive drill-down analysis

## Key Business Questions Answered
1. Which product category generates the highest sales?
2. Which market (region) contributes the most to overall revenue?
3. What shipping method do customers prefer — and does it affect sales volume?
4. Which countries and states are the top revenue contributors?

## Key Insights
- Sales are concentrated in a small number of high-performing categories, suggesting an opportunity to rationalize the product portfolio
- Market-level analysis reveals geographic revenue imbalances that can guide regional marketing investment
- Ship mode distribution indicates customer delivery preferences that can inform logistics and fulfilment strategy
